Trader

A fully automated trading bot for the Binance crypto exchange.

  • Trading on 15 minute interval
  • Buy if RSI was below 30 and rises again over 30
  • On buy, invest a third of current equity if available
  • Stop loss and take profit at 1.8 times ATR
  • Sell if stop loss or take profit is reached or if RSI reaches 70
  • Only buy after RSI has reached 70 again some time in the past

This project is open source on GitHub. All assets are currently simulated. Please note that although the exchange rates reflect the real market, the real profit rate may not be as advertised as it is assumed that supply and demand are always available at the current market price. Trading fees are taken into consideration.
